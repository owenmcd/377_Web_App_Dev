BLACKJACK

In the game of BlackJack you are playing against the dealer. First you place your bets then you are given two cards. Now you have the option to either "hit" or "stand", hit means to be given another card from the deck and stand means to flip your cards. Your goal is to get the sum of your cards close to 21 without going over. If you go over, you bust which means the dealer wins. When you flip your cards if your cards are higher than the dealers you win the amount of money that you bet. If the dealer has higher cards and does not bust, you lose your money.
